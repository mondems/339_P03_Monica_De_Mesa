Branding Colours

warm tones “can spark a variety of emotions ranging from comfort and warmth to hostility and anger” (http://www.arttherapyblog.com/online/color-psychology-psychologica-effects-of-colors/) As my creative intentions are to connect with others on an emotional level, I wanted to incorporate a warm colour in contrast with black and white tones. As I strive to convey relatable and thought provoking emotions in my work, adding a warm yellow colour was important for my branding.
